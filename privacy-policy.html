Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 19, 2025

My Daily Diary is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how your information is handled within the app.

1. Information Collection

We do not collect any personal data from users. All notes, reminders, and attachments are stored locally on your device and are not transmitted to any server.

2. Permissions

The app may request the following permissions:

  • Storage: To save and access your attachments (images, PDFs).
  • Notifications: To send you reminders for your notes.

3. Data Security

Since no data is collected or transmitted, there is no risk of your data being accessed or misused by us. We advise you to backup your data regularly.

4. Third-party Services

This app does not use any third-party analytics or advertising services.
